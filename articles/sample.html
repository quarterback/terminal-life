The Interface is the Institution

By Tristen B. Vornanen — March 2025

Governance has become a UX problem—intentionally, structurally, and perhaps permanently.

Systems don’t break, they reveal. What we mistake for digital efficiency is often just institutional abdication behind a button.

The interface is now where law is interpreted. Where service delivery is judged. Where failure hides inside frictionless flows.

The interface didn’t glitch. It obeyed power.

To resist this means understanding systems not as broken—but as functioning precisely as intended.
